# **Law Enforcement Quick Reference**

<Aside type="note" title="Signals">
**Signal 100**: Hold all but emergency traffic  
**Signal 60**: Drugs  
**Signal 11**: Running radar  
**Code Zero**: Game crash  
**Code 4**: Under control  
**Code 5**: Felony stop or high-risk stop
</Aside>

## **Response Codes**

<Aside type="tip" title="Response Reference">
**Code 1** – Routine, follow all traffic laws  
**Code 2** – Urgent, no lights or sirens unless needed  
**Code 3** – Emergency, lights and sirens active
</Aside>
